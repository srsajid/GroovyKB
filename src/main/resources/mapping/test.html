Details

Lenovo Phab 2 Tablet

AUGMENTED ENTERTAINMENT WITH DOLBY TECHNOLOGY

With the Lenovo Phab 2, every commute is like a trip to the cinema. Experience true immersive multimedia with the stunning 6.4" HD screen and incredible Dolby Atmos audio. Record amazing 360-degree multichannel sound with Dolby 5.1 Audio Capture. Or simply enhance any video or photo with the 13 MP fast-focus camera.

6.4" HD IPS Screen

With a huge 6.4” HD screen and amazing Dolby sound, the Phab 2 smartphone is like having your own personal a cinema in your pocket—watch videos, chat with friends and colleagues.

Cinematic Dolby Atmos 

Be blown away with Dolby Atmos. The surround sound puts you at the heart of the action every time you watch your favorite movies or play the hottest games on your Phab 2.

Lifelike Recorded Sound With Dolby Audio Capture 5.1

Our newest generations of Phab devices are the world's first smartphones with Dolby Audio Capture 5.1. Now you can record amazing audio with 360-degree immersive quality. Coupled with Dolby technology, the 3 built-in microphones capture amazingly nuanced video sound. Plus, with 360-degree noise cancellation, conference call and interview modes let you tailor your recordings for maximum clarity. With the lifelike sounds, your videos really come to life.

Great Photos, Videos, & Selfies

Leave your camera at home and still capture breathtaking photos, thanks to the Phab 2's rear 13 MP camera. Pull all your friends into the frame for a groupfie with the 5 MP front camera, featuring an 85-degree wide-angled lens.

Packed With Power & Plenty of Storage

At the heart of the Phab 2 is a fast 1.3 GHz quad-core processor, 3 GB RAM, and 32 GB of storage, along with a microSD slot. That means smoother performance even when multitasking and ample room for all of your movies, photos, and apps .

Enough Juice for All-Day Play

The Phab 2 features a powerful 4,050 mAh battery that gives you longer times between charges—so you'll always have enough juice to shoot, share, chat, and play. Enjoy up 32 hours of talk-time and a standby capacity.

Unlocked for More Freedom

Phab2 is unlocked for AT&T, T-Mobile, and compatible GSM networks so you can switch carriers and keep your phone. Just simply swap SIM cards (Micro SIM). Verizon and Sprint not supported.
